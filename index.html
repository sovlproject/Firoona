Firoona

iOS Storyboard Drawing App for film, game, and animation design.

What is Firoona?

Firoona allows you to draw your storyboards, plan scenes, and design game or film visuals on your iPhone or iPad. Easy to use, with scene previews, onion skinning, and export features.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which devices does Firoona support?

Firoona runs on iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later. macOS support is coming soon.

Can I sync my projects across devices?

Yes, Firoona uses iCloud to keep your projects synced across all your Apple devices signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I export my storyboard scenes?

You can export individual scenes or all scenes as images from within the app. Each scene is exported with a filename containing your project name and scene number.
