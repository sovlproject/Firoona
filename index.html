Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync projects across devices?

Sign in with the same Apple ID on your iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Projects automatically sync via iCloud.

How can I export a scene?

Tap the Export Scene button in the toolbar to save a PNG of your drawing. You can also export all scenes at once.

Which devices are supported?

Firoona works on iPhone, iPad, and Mac (Apple Silicon or M1+). PencilKit features require devices that support Apple Pencil.
