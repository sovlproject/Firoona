F.A.Q.

How do I sync projects? +

Firona uses iCloud to keep your storyboards in sync across iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Just make sure you're logged into the same Apple ID.

Can I import photos into scenes? +

Yes! You can add photos to any scene, rotate, and scale them. Once placed, they become a permanent part of your storyboard frame.

What export formats are supported? +

Export individual scenes as PNG, or your entire project as a Video (MP4) to see your story in motion.
