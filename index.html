About Firoona

Firoona is a versatile storyboard drawing app for iOS, designed for filmmakers, game developers, and creatives. Plan your scenes, sketch ideas, and organize your storyboards effortlessly.

Features include multiple scenes, onion skinning, easy project management, and export options for your storyboard images.

FAQ

Can I export my scenes?

Absolutely. Each scene can be exported as a high-resolution image to share or use in your projects.

Does it sync across devices?

Yes, Firoona can sync your projects across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac using iCloud.

What devices are supported?

Firoona works on iPhone, iPad, and Mac running the latest iOS/macOS versions.
