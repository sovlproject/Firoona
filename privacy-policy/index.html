Privacy Policy for Firona

Last updated: January 2026

Firona (“the App”) is developed and operated by Firona. Your privacy is important to us.

Information Collection and Use

Firona does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No user accounts
  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No advertising

All content created in the app remains on your device unless you choose to export it.

In-App Purchases

Firona offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock premium features. Payments are processed securely by Apple. Firona does not receive or store payment information.

Photos & Files Access

If you export content to Photos or Files, access is used only to perform the requested action. No data is collected.

Children’s Privacy

Firona does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at contact@firona.app.
